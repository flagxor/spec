Result Types¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef3055#1{{}}\mathdef3055{result type} & \href{../text/types.html#text-resulttype}{\mathtt{resulttype}} &::=& (t{:}\href{../text/types.html#text-functype}{\mathtt{result}})^? &\Rightarrow& [t^?] \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Note

In future versions of WebAssembly, this scheme may be extended to support multiple results or more general result types.

Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef3055#1{{}}\mathdef3055{parameter} & \def\mathdef3125#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3125{(}~~\def\mathdef3126#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3126{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3127#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3127{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3128#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3128{(}~~\def\mathdef3129#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3129{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3130#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3130{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef3055#1{{}}\mathdef3055{result} & \def\mathdef3131#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3131{(}~~\def\mathdef3132#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3132{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3133#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3133{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3134#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3134{(}~~\def\mathdef3135#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3135{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3136#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3136{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
